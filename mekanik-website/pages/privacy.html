Vehicle Management

Mekanik's vehicle management feature allows users to store and track their vehicle's service history and maintenance in one place. Users can access their vehicle's information, schedule repairs and receive notifications for upcoming services. This feature helps users stay organized, ensuring their vehicle is always in top condition.

Reviews and Ratings

Mekanik's review and rating system enables users to leave feedback for mechanics, spare parts dealers, and services. This feature helps build trust and accountability within the community, ensuring users make informed decisions and get quality services.

Spare Parts Catalog

Mekanik's comprehensive spare parts catalog allows users to browse and purchase genuine spare parts from authorized dealers. Users can search by vehicle make, model, or part name, and filter results by price, brand, and availability. This feature ensures users get the right parts for their vehicle, with fast delivery or pickup options.
